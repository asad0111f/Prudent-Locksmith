# Google Ads Structure (Hamilton-First)

## Account Assumptions
- Single account for Prudent Locksmith and Garage Services.
- Separate campaigns by service intent and urgency.
- /lp pages are used for ads and are noindex by default.

## Campaign Naming Convention
- `PPC | {City} | {Intent} | {Network}`
  - Example: `PPC | Hamilton | Garage Door Emergency | Search`

## Location Targeting Notes
- Primary: Hamilton, ON
- Secondary: Burlington, Oakville, Mississauga, Milton, Brampton, Stoney Creek, Ancaster, Dundas, Waterdown
- Use radius + city targeting for tighter control during launch.

## Budget Split (Launch)
- Emergency: 40%
- Non-emergency core services: 50%
- Brand: 10%

## Bid Strategy (Launch Recommendation)
- Start with manual CPC or Max Clicks with a strict cap.
- After conversions are stable, move to Max Conversions.
