# Content & Brand Placeholders

## Required Assets
- Final logo (SVG + PNG)
- Real service photos for each category/service
- Brand color confirmation
- Brand typography confirmation

## Business Details
- Confirmed service area list
- Business hours (including emergency availability)
- Licensing details (where required)
- Insurance details (where applicable)

## Social Proof
- Review screenshots or quotes with permission
- Average rating and review count (if available)

## Operations
- Preferred contact email inbox for leads
- Dispatch phone line verification
- Warranty/guarantee details
