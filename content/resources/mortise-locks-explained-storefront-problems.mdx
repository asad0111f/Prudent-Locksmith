Mortise locks are common on commercial storefront doors because they’re durable and secure. When they fail, access and security can be impacted quickly.

## Common mortise lock issues
- Key turns but latch won’t retract
- Stiff or sticky action
- Cylinder wear or key breakage

## Typical fixes
- Adjust door alignment and latch
- Service internal components
- Replace the mortise cylinder if worn
